# Business Intelligence Requirements

## Stakeholders
1. **Hospital Management** - Strategic decision making
2. **Department Heads** - Operational improvements
3. **Quality Assurance Team** - Service monitoring
4. **IT Department** - System performance

## Decision Support Needs
1. **Service Quality Monitoring**
   - Real-time department performance
   - Patient satisfaction trends
   - Issue identification and resolution

2. **Resource Allocation**
   - Identify underperforming departments
   - Staff training needs assessment
   - Infrastructure improvement planning

3. **Compliance & Audit**
   - Business rule violation tracking
   - Data integrity monitoring
   - Security audit trails

## Reporting Frequency
- **Real-time:** Department dashboards
- **Daily:** Feedback volume and status
- **Weekly:** Performance summaries
- **Monthly:** Trend analysis and recommendations
- **Quarterly:** Strategic review and planning
