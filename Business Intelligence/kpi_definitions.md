# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## 1. Patient Satisfaction Score
- **Formula:** AVG(RATING) per department
- **Target:** ≥ 4.0/5.0
- **Frequency:** Daily monitoring, weekly reporting
- **Owner:** Department Heads

## 2. Feedback Response Rate
- **Formula:** (RESOLVED feedback / TOTAL feedback) × 100
- **Target:** ≥ 90% within 72 hours
- **Frequency:** Daily monitoring
- **Owner:** Quality Assurance Team

## 3. Positive Feedback Percentage
- **Formula:** (Feedback with RATING ≥ 4 / TOTAL feedback) × 100
- **Target:** ≥ 80%
- **Frequency:** Weekly reporting
- **Owner:** Hospital Management

## 4. Business Rule Compliance Rate
- **Formula:** (Allowed operations / Total attempts) × 100
- **Target:** 100% compliance
- **Frequency:** Real-time monitoring
- **Owner:** IT Department

## 5. Audit Trail Completeness
- **Formula:** (Logged operations / Actual operations) × 100
- **Target:** 100%
- **Frequency:** Daily verification
- **Owner:** Security Team
